# FlankerTech Solutions

> **Building India's Skills-First Tech Talent Pipeline**

FlankerTech Solutions is a platform designed to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry expectations. It enables students to gain real-world project experience, receive mentor-validated credentials, and build proof-of-work portfolios while helping companies discover pre-assessed, industry-ready talent.

The platform creates a transparent ecosystem where skills are demonstrated through real projects instead of traditional resumes alone.

##  Features

* Modern responsive landing page
* Startup overview and mission
* Skills-first hiring model
* Internship ecosystem showcase
* Skill Tier Framework (Brown, Silver & Gold)
* Business model and vision
* Contact and partnership section
* Smooth animations and modern UI

##  Vision

FlankerTech Solutions aims to become India's trusted platform for skill validation by transforming technical ability into verified proof-of-work. Through industry-backed projects, mentor guidance, and credentialed internships, the platform empowers students while providing companies with a reliable pipeline of job-ready talent.

##  Mission

* Bridge the gap between education and employment.
* Replace resume-based hiring with skill-based validation.
* Build trusted proof-of-work portfolios.
* Connect students, mentors, universities, and companies on one platform.
* Reduce hiring risk for organizations while improving graduate employability.
